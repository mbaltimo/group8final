Why did we choose this technology?

ChatGPT is one of the most widely used AI tools right now; students use it to study and get writing feedback, businesses use it handle customer service and generate content, and developers use it to help write and troubleshoot code.

Why does it matter?

However, that reach also comes with real problems. ChatGPT can give wrong answers in a convincing way, making misinformation a genuine risk. The AI system reflects biases from the data it was trained on, often in ways users do not notice. Some students use it in ways that undermine their own learning. Additionally, some job industries have begun laying off their human employees in favor of AI tools to complete tasks.

The problems and benefits presented by ChatGPT are actively playing out, this presents us with concrete information rather than a hypothetical scenario.
