Real-World Applications of Generative AI

Education

Generative AI tools like ChatGPT are commonly used by students and educators alike to complete tasks such as brainstorming, tutoring, writing and coding support, research, and studying. To further support the education industry, OpenAI has developed an additional platform specifically designed for education, called ChatGPT Edu. This tool is designed specifically for colleges and universities to use AI across campus with added security and administrative controls.

Education affects everyone, and Generative AI can provide an easier way of learning by providing students with quick topic explanations, examples to practice with, and give feedback when needed. Generative AI can also help educators by creating lesson plans, quizzes, and study material so they have more time to focus on interacting with students.

While ChatGPT and other Generative AI tools can be helpful to the education industry, it also presents significant social challenges. Instead of using AI tools to aid their learning experience, students are overelying on them to complete their work. Major concerns regarding, cheating, plagiarism, privacy, and potentially biased or incorrect information have risen. UN Agency UNESCO urges educational facilities to implement clear guidelines on AI usage to ensure it's used in a responsible and human-centered manner.
